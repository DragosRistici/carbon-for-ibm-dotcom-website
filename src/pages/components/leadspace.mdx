## Lead space left-aligned

This is the most commonly used Lead space style. The left-aligned type draws the users' attention to the Lead space messaging immediately. Used in marketing and product pages, among others.

There are four available height sizes:

- Super (640px)
- Tall (560px)
- Medium (480px)
- Short (320px)

For detailed guidelines around usage, also reference the [Lead space pattern page](/patterns/lead-space).

## Lead space centered

This option has 4 empty columns on the left and right to accommodate pages that have content positioned in the center of the 16-column grid. Used in the upcoming Learn page template and other long-form reading experiences.

![Lead space centered](../../images/pattern/leadspace/Leadspace_Demo_LeftAligned.jpg)

| Description    | Short copy      | No                                    | M (120 / 150)                                  | –               |

## Color themes

There are two color themes to choose from when using Lead space:

### Dark theme

Use `Dark (gray-100)` when the background image has many shadows, is dimly lit, or uses a dark color palette. The gradient overlay will render dark and the text will be white.

![Dark theme](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48327647/112467050-3715e480-8d6f-11eb-9ff7-f853ba836a93.jpg)

<Caption>Dark Lead space – Super – 640px height at xlg 1312px break point</Caption>
<br />

### Light theme

Use `Light (white)` when the background image is well-lit or brightly colored. The gradient overlay will render white and the text will be gray-100.

![Light_theme](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48327647/112467678-071b1100-8d70-11eb-8fc9-069f91c24563.jpg)

<Caption>Light Lead space – Super – 640px height at xlg 1312px break point</Caption>

## Type styles

### Lead space left-aligned

There are two expressive type styles to choose from when using Lead space left-aligned:

- `$expressive-heading-06` is recommended for pages that surface detailed, productive content to users such as product pages.
- `$display-01` is recommended for pages that have rich editorial, expressive moments such as marketing or campaign pages.

![Lead space with $expressive-heading-06](../../images/pattern/leadspace/LeadSpace_TypeStyles_$expressive-heading-06.jpg)

<Caption>
  $expressive-heading-06 can be used to highlight the name of the product,
  offering or title of topic. This option is optimal if using all Lead space and
  components together. 
</Caption>

<br />

![Lead space with $display-01](../../images/pattern/leadspace/LeadSpace_TypeStyles_$display-01.jpg)

<Caption>
  $display-01 can be used to celebrate strong, editorial messaging.
</Caption>

### Lead space centered

There are three expressive type styles to choose from when using Lead space centered:

- `$expressive-heading-06` is recommended for pages that surface detailed, productive content to users such as product pages.
- `$display-01` and `$expressive-heading-05` is recommended for pages that have rich editorial, expressive moments such as marketing or campaign pages.

## Design and functional specifications

### Lead space left-aligned

![$expressive-heading-06 at 1312 xlg breakpoint](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48327647/112470936-ee145f00-8d73-11eb-94f9-9849b9b68e88.png)

<Caption>Example: $expressive-heading-06 at xlg breakpoint (1312px) for a Medium (480px height) lead space</Caption>

<br />

1. **Heading** <br/>Type: $expressive-heading-06, color: $text-01, width: calc(8col – 32px)
1. **Content spacer** <br/>Minimum space between lead space heading and short copy: 96px
1. **Short copy** \*optional <br/>Type: $expressive-heading-03, color: $text-01, width: calc(6col – 32px)
1. **Button group** <br/>Use the Button group component
1. **Gradient (applied over image)** <br/>Color: black (#000000), linear: left to right <br/>Opacity: 1 at start, 0.4 at 50%, 0 at 75% of total container width
1. **Background image** \*optional <br/>Cover, width: 100% of container for all breakpoints. For detailed guidelines, visit [the imagery section on the Lead space pattern page](/patterns/lead-space#imagery).

### Lead space centered

![leadspace_specs_dark_centered_demo](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/48327647/112479811-0d17ee80-8d7e-11eb-9858-592cd6c2ebe7.png)

<Caption>Example: $display-01 at max breakpoint (1584px) for a Super (640px) lead space</Caption>

<br />

1. **Heading** <br/>Type: $display-01, color: $text-01, width: calc(8col – 32px)
2. **Short copy** \*optional <br/>$expressive-heading-03, color: $text-01, width: calc(6col – 32px)
3. **Button group** <br/>Use the Button group component
4. **Content bottom spacer** <br/>Height: $layout-07 + $layout-06 with background image, <br/>or $layout-07 without background image
5. **Gradient (applied over image)** <br/>Color: black (#000000), linear: top to bottom, <br/>Opacity: 1 at start, 0.6 at 256px from bottom, 0 at 160px from bottom
6. **Background image** \*optional <br/>Cover, width: 100% of container for all breakpoints. <br/>Image position: center bottom.
